# Prompts y Continuidad Creativa

## Key visual

Prompt base usado:

```text
Create an original, entertaining football key visual for SIFUP, a web app for organizing weekly amateur football matches, players, teams, payments, and WhatsApp coordination. Vibrant football pitch energy mixed with urban Latin American amateur football culture; abstract stadium lights, pitch lines, confetti-like modular shapes, player silhouettes in motion, ball movement trails, small UI-inspired payment/check/team motifs integrated as graphic details. Bold contemporary sports campaign illustration, colorful modular graphic system, poster-like composition with characterful typography. Use emerald green, golden yellow, electric blue, magenta, red, white, and black accents. Include only the word "SIFUP" if text is needed. Avoid FIFA, World Cup, official logos, trophy, mascot, slogan, flags and sponsor marks.
```

## Logo transparente exploratorio

Prompt base usado:

```text
Create a standalone original SIFUP wordmark logo inspired by a bold football campaign identity: chunky custom display letters, playful sports energy, emerald green main lettering, white internal highlights, black/dark shadow extrusion, small yellow and cyan accent strokes. The logo should feel like amateur football, rankings, energy, and team culture. Use only the word "SIFUP". Generate over a flat chroma background for removal. No FIFA references, no official tournament styling, no trophy, no flags, no sponsor marks.
```

## Direccion para proximas piezas

Para mantener continuidad, pedir siempre:

- Futbol amateur organizado.
- Cancha de noche.
- Grafica de barrio bien producida.
- Letras pesadas y angulares.
- Verde SIFUP como color base.
- Amarillo, cyan, magenta y rojo como acentos.
- Textura, polvo, energia y trazos de velocidad.
- Legibilidad para celular.

Evitar:

- Look corporativo generico.
- Gradientes suaves sin caracter.
- Landing page de marketing.
- Logos oficiales de torneos.
- Recursos que parezcan FIFA, World Cup o sponsors.
